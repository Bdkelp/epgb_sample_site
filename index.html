FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about selling your valuables

How much will I get for my gold jewelry?

+

The value depends on the weight, karat purity, and current gold market price. We test your items on-site and provide an immediate cash offer based on the live gold price. Most customers find we pay 10-20% more than competitors.

Do I need an appointment?

+

No appointment is necessary! Walk-ins are always welcome during business hours. However, if you prefer to schedule a time, feel free to call us at (915) 626-5661 to ensure we have dedicated time for your evaluation.

Will you buy broken or damaged jewelry?

+

Absolutely! We buy all gold jewelry regardless of condition. Broken chains, single earrings, bent rings - we purchase it all based on the precious metal content, not the appearance.

How do you determine the value of diamonds?

+

We evaluate diamonds based on the 4 C's: Cut, Clarity, Color, and Carat weight. Our certified appraisers use professional equipment to assess each stone and provide you with a fair market offer.

Do you buy watches that don't work?

+

Yes! We purchase luxury watches like Rolex in any condition - working or non-working. Even damaged watches have significant value due to their precious metal content and brand prestige.

What do I need to bring to sell?

+

Just bring a valid government-issued photo ID (driver's license, passport, or state ID) and the items you wish to sell. We handle everything else on-site.

How quickly will I receive payment?

+

Immediately! Once you accept our offer, we pay you in cash on the spot. The entire process typically takes 15-30 minutes from evaluation to payment.

Are you licensed and insured?

+

Yes! We are fully licensed, bonded, and insured. We also maintain an A+ rating with the Better Business Bureau and comply with all Texas state regulations for precious metal dealers.
